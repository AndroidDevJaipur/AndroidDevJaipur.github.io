Welcome to ADJ

AndroidDev Jaipur was started as an initiative to create awareness among young students about the possibilities of Android development to provide a platform to discuss new technologies and help individuals get started with android.

Goals

Our Objectives

Development

The community aims at making individuals grow in their respective field of interest, and polish them as individuals

Open Source Dev

Through enthralling open source projects learn how to make contributions and gain skills on the way.

Motivation

Helping out the community for self satisfaction and and motivating individuals out there into exploring Android

Networking

While gaining experience meet new people with similar interests and which will help you grow further in life.
