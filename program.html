KOTLIN EVERYWHERE

This is a series of community-led events, where you can learn the essentials and best practices of ​ Kotlin​ in Android​ Google Cloud Platform,​ and multi-platform development.

To help you dive deeper into Kotlin and share the love, Google and JetBrains are helping organize a series of community-driven events all across the globe called Kotlin/Everywhere​ . They aim to help learn the essentials and best practices of using Kotlin, deploying it in every domain and all popular platforms, be it back-end or front-end on Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, Linux, or the web!

MEET-UP 0

This was our first ever meetup held in Manipal University Jaipur. The agenda was to meet with the variety of people that are involved in our community and get their ideas on what they would prefer from the side of moderators’ side that may contribute to the betterment of everyone who’s a member. The meeting also resulted in a group of tech enthusiasts meeting like minded people with various backgrounds and sharing both personal stories as well as recent updates in the field of development. There was a small networking break where attendees were offered light snacks.

The conclusion of the meetup was on a positive note where it was decided that the team of AndroidDev Jaipur will be aiding the members on its telegram group by hosting events and meetups along with open source projects for developers to partake in and learn from them.
